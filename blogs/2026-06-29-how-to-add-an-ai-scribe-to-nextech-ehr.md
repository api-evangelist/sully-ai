This guide explains three methods for integrating an AI scribe into Nextech EHR: Nextech's native Cora Scribe, cross-EHR scribes, or workforce platforms like Sully.ai. Cora Scribe offers specialty-tuned documentation for ophthalmology, while workforce platforms extend beyond note-taking to include coding, claims submission, scheduling, and triage.
